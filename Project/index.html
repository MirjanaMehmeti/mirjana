About Us

Founded in 2024 by Mirjana Mehmeti, Cat Club has come a long way from its beginnings as a small blog. When Mirjana first started out, her passion for helping cat owners drove her to start this website, and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a thriving online community.

Mirjana's journey began when she adopted her first cat, Whiskers. She realized that there were many like her who wanted reliable information and a community to share their experiences with.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the lives of cats and their owners by providing trusted, comprehensive resources and fostering a community of cat enthusiasts. We strive to be the go-to resource for cat care information, offering advice on everything from nutrition to behavioral issues, and ensuring that every cat receives the love and care they deserve.

Our History

Cat Club started as a small blog and has grown into a comprehensive resource for cat lovers. We share articles, tips, and stories to help you better understand and care for your feline friends. Over the years, we have expanded our team to include experts in veterinary medicine, animal behavior, and experienced cat owners who contribute their knowledge and insights.

What We Offer

Cat Club offers a wide range of resources including detailed articles on cat health, behavior tips, training advice, and product reviews. We also feature stories from our community, celebrating the bond between cats and their humans. Our articles are written by experts and are designed to provide you with the most accurate and up-to-date information available.

Training and Behavior

Understanding your cat's behavior is key to a harmonious relationship. We provide tips on training, behavior correction, and understanding your cat's body language. Our resources are designed to help you address common behavioral issues and build a strong bond with your feline friend.

Adoption Opportunities

At Cat Club, we believe every cat deserves a loving home. We have partnered with local shelters and rescue organizations to feature cats that are available for adoption. You can find many adorable cats looking for their forever homes on our Adoption Page. By clicking the button on the right, you can explore the profiles of these cats and find the perfect companion for your family.
